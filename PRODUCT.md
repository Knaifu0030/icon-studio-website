# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Parents in and around Hejjala Gate / Kumbalgodu (Bengaluru 562109) looking for dance or fitness classes for their children, plus teenagers and adults seeking dance training or movement-based fitness (Zumba, aerobics, yoga) for themselves. They arrive from a poster sighting, a WhatsApp forward, or a local Google search, mostly on mid-range Android phones. Their job: decide quickly whether this academy feels credible and premium, then enquire with minimal friction.

## Product Purpose

A single-page marketing website for A ICON Dance Academy, a new dance and fitness academy near Ganesha Temple, Hejjala Gate, Bengaluru. Success = a visitor taps "Enrol Now" and lands in a prefilled WhatsApp conversation (or calls one of the two numbers). Classes are starting soon and seats are limited; the site converts curiosity into enquiries.

## Positioning

A premium performing-arts academy in a neighbourhood of tuition-centre templates — dance taught as confidence, discipline and stage presence, not just steps. "Where Moves Create Memories."

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: Enrol Now → prefilled WhatsApp enquiry (wa.me/919980340996).
- Secondary CTA: Call 99803 40996 / 81230 33707; tertiary: Explore Classes (anchor scroll).
- The line a visitor remembers: Discover. Learn. Transform.
- Belief ladder: (1) this place is professional and premium, (2) there is a style for me/my child (9 programmes, all ages), (3) it builds confidence and stage presence, not just fitness, (4) it's nearby and easy to reach, (5) seats are limited — enquire now.
- Proof on hand: NONE beyond the poster. Do not fabricate testimonials, ratings, student counts, awards, founding year, fees, schedules, or trainer names. Where specifics are needed, say "Contact the academy for current batch timings and fee details."

## Brand Personality

Aspirational, artistic, disciplined. Cinematic theatre-lighting mood: obsidian black, champagne gold used selectively, warm ivory type, controlled deep-red/burgundy accents. Feels like a luxury performing-arts studio / theatre production, warm and welcoming to beginners of all ages. British/Indian English; always "Enrol", never "Enroll".

## Anti-references

Not a neighbourhood tuition-centre template, generic gym site, or AI landing page. No neon club styling, cheap gold gradients, glowing text, purple SaaS gradients, clip-art dancer silhouettes, emoji-as-icons, fake counters/reviews/social links, glassmorphism-by-default, or the full poster pasted in as the design.

## Design Principles

1. Theatre, not template — every section art-directed like a stage production; gold is a spotlight, not a wash.
2. Movement is the brand — motion, imagery and type express rhythm; all of it degrades gracefully under prefers-reduced-motion.
3. Nothing invented — only poster-verified facts; unknowns route to "contact the academy".
4. Two taps to a conversation — every fold offers a real path to WhatsApp or a phone call; contact data lives in one config file.
5. Premium but light — fast on mid-range Android; images lazy and responsive, JS minimal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Semantic HTML, correct heading order, keyboard navigation with visible focus, body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, descriptive alt text (empty alt for decorative), aria-expanded on the mobile menu, visible form labels with helpful validation, prefers-reduced-motion honoured everywhere, comfortable touch targets (~44px).
